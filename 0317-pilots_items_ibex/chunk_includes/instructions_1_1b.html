We will show you a sentence and two cards that pictorially display facts about a situation.
Your task is to choose the card that best illustrates the sentence. The examples will look like this:

The bags are heavy.

press D press K

As you can see, there is a catch: one of the cards is completely visible, while the other is blurry so you cannot see it completely.
If you think the visible card is a good enough illustration of the sentence, click on it. Otherwise, assume that the blurred card is a better option.

In the example above, the visible card is indeed a good illustration. You would thus choose the left card, either by clicking directly on the card or by pressing the D key.
