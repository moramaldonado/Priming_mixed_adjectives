We will show you a sentence and two cards that pictorially display facts about a situation.
Your task is to choose the card that best illustrates the sentence. The examples will look like this:

The bags are heavy.


Press D Press K

In the example above, you would thus choose the left card, either by clicking directly on the card or by pressing the D key.

If you're not sure what card to choose, please go with your gut intuition.
